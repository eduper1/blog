How to write a mail.

November 01, 2021, by Eduper

Communication has indeed been there ever since the dawn of humanity, and it has been evolving along with us. Mail writing is the modern communication of our era, and it is not an exaggeration to say mail writing is a crucial skill to append to your CV.

Two decades ago, to write a mail one needed a pen and paper, perhaps a candle, during nighttime. All that changed as humans marked the kick-off of the new millennium, electricity became cheaper, what we call now “Giant” techno companies emerged, personal computers were rolled all over the world, eradicating computer illiteracy was the new theme of the world. Now that computers are everywhere: at offices, homes, and even inside our pockets or handbags, communication barriers have been diluted with the rise of social media applications such as WeChat, Facebook, Whatsapp, Signal, etc.


Mail writing is replaced by E-mail (Electronic mail) which was increasingly adapted by government offices, companies, and everyone who thought the necessity to board the new wave of communication. Unlike other instant messaging apps, an e-mail account is used for official duties. As days passed, E-Mail became the go-to means of communication, for instance, the government will require an e-mail address when applying for a service, universities will ask for an e-mail address to update applicants on their admission progress, moreover, even social media apps required E-Mail to prove the identity of their users.

Mail Format.

Below is the format used in mail writing.

To begin with the address. There are two addresses: the writer’s address which appears on the top right-hand corner of the page, and the receiver’s address which appears on the left-hand side of the paper – below the writer’s address.

Secondly, the salutation, salutation refers to the greetings which introduce the body of the letter. In mail writing, salutation depends on whom you are writing to; refer to the receiver’s gender or name if you know that, else, salutation: like “Dear Sir/Madam” will be enough.

Thirdly, the reference and the body. The reference is the header sentence of the letter, and it indicates the purpose of the letter while the body carries the purpose of the letter in detail. The body part is divided into three: the introduction, the aim, and the ending.


Lastly, the ending, ending consists of a subscription and the writer’s signature. For subscription phrases: like ”Yours faithfully or your respectful” is used, while the signature is handwritten and comes before the writer’s name.


It is crucial to notice the difference when writing an e-mail and a mail(letter). E-mail do not require the sender’s address or receiver’s address block to comprise, because the receiver’s block is replaced by (To), which needs the e-mail address of the receiver, and the recipient will automatically see the sender’s information such as their name or company’s name and e-mail address. Also, the reference is replaced by (Subject) and the sender’s signature by electronic signature(sometimes not necessary).
